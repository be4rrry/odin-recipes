Ingredients

  • 1 lb sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 lb lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 garlic cloves - note from the edihttps://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Fi.pinimg.com%2F736x%2F87%2Fda%2F27%2F87da27379b44d4ac7515a56f59f8cfed.jpg&f=1&nofb=1 crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1 tsp italian seasonings
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 tbsp chropped fresh parsley
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 oz ricotta
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 lb sliced mozz
  • 3/4 cup grated parm

Steps

  1. In a large dutch oven, cook your sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic on medium head until fragrant and brown. Add in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and some water. Add your seasonings, listed below. Simmer, while covered, for about an hour and a half, making sure to stir every now and then.
  2. Bring a large, liberally salted pot of water to a boil. Cook the noods for about 8-10 mins. Drain the noods, and rinse them with cold water. In a bowl add the ricoota with egg, parsley, and 1/2 tsp salt.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  4. Assemble by spreading the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 in baking dish. Arrange 6 noods over the meat sauce. Spead with 1/2 of the ricotta mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozz cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups of the meaty sauce over the mozz, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup of parm. Repeat until out of stuff. Cover it with foil. Spray with cooking spray to stop sticking, or don't touch foil to cheese >:(
  5. Bake for 25 mins. Remove foil, and bake for 25 more mins. Let it cool before eating. I am not responsible for burnt tongues. Or anything else burnt for that matter.
